How long does a typical makeover session take

The duration depends on the type of makeover. For example, bridal makeovers usually take around 2-3 hours to ensure every detail is perfect, while party and event makeovers typically take about 1-1.5 hours.

What products do you use, and are they suitable for sensitive skin?

I use high-quality, professional-grade makeup products that are suitable for various skin types, including sensitive skin. If you have specific concerns or allergies, please let me know so I can make any necessary adjustments.

Can I book a custom theme or specific look for my makeover?

Absolutely! My Custom Theme Makeover service is perfect for creating unique, theme-based looks. Let me know the event theme or style you have in mind, and I’ll tailor the makeover to match it perfectly..

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

It’s best to book as early as possible, especially for bridal and large events, to secure your desired date. For smaller events or parties, at least 1-2 weeks’ notice is recommended to ensure availability.

Do you travel to the event location, and is there an additional charge for that?

Yes, I offer on-location services for your convenience. Travel fees may apply depending on the distance from my base location. Feel free to ask for a detailed quote based on your venue!

How much does bridal makeup cost in Narela?

Bridal makeup costs vary based on the package, products used (HD/Airbrush), and additional services like hairstyling and draping. Contact us for a customized quote!

What is the difference between HD makeup and airbrush makeup?

HD makeup gives a natural, flawless finish for high-definition cameras, while airbrush makeup is lightweight, lasts longer, and is waterproof—perfect for brides.

Do you offer makeup trials before the wedding?

Yes! We provide makeup trials so you can experience your bridal look before the big day.
